Characters

Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main protagonist of One Piece. He is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and possesses the power of the Gum-Gum Fruit, which gives him the ability to stretch his body like rubber.

Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is a swordsman and a member of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is known for his incredible swordsmanship and his dream to become the world's greatest swordsman.
